Projects

Hangman Game

A hangman game implemented using Python. It follows the same rules; the player must guess the word by trying one letter at a time. Every incorrect guess leads to a part of the hangman being added. Given only seven guesses, the player loses once every hangman part is complete.

What's your Type?

“What’s Your Type” is a typing game made using Python. A player is presented with words that they must correctly type into the terminal with the following score system:

  • 3-letter words worth two points
  • 4-letter words worth five points
  • 5-letter words worth seven points

Each player’s score will be displayed in the leaderboard including their total time. The player’s words per minute is also presented after every playthrough.

Greenvale Gone Gray

“Greenvale Gone Gray” is a short story-based strategy game made in Java, with its UI utilizing CSS styling. Its goal focuses on the concept of optimization in data science. The player is tasked with optimizing the costs of mitigation projects in order to minimize Greenvale city's severe pollution footprint.

CafeBara

“CafeBara” is your all-in-one cloud-based cafe app made in Java and styled using CSS. It simulates a seamless cafe experience for ordering premium drinks and fresh pastries. It also provides table reservation services as well as exclusive perks and benefits for VIP users.
